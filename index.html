Scouting

You may recognize scouts for "being prepared" or outdoorsy, but there is much more to it than that. The mission of scouting is to preparing youth for life! To prepare scouts to make good choices, the scouting program focuses on character development, leadership development, citizenship training, and personal fitness. Our scouting units are blessed to be part of Community Bible Church... so preparing youth for life definitely includes their eternal life! Scouting provides a great opportunity to practice and discuss our duty to God.
